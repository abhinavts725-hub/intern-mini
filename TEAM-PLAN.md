## Leadership

| Role                           | Responsibilities                                                                                                     |
| ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **President** (Non-Technical)  | Assigns roles, manages deadlines, collects daily logs, ensures everyone commits to GitHub, prepares final submission |
| **Vice President** (Technical) | Leads all technical decisions, reviews code, guides technical members, oversees model training                       |

> President focuses on **management & coordination** — no coding required.
> VP handles all **technical oversight**.
